Children Section

The Raja Tun Uda Library, Shah Alam Children's Library is located in the lower right wing and is dedicated to children under the age of 10. In addition to a wide selection of children's books that you can borrow, there are various places that offer interesting places such as Reading Tree, Creative Zone, Interactive Zone and Cyber Zone.




Reading Tree is a library concept for children to read in a relaxed atmosphere. Large tree replicas are built for children to relax and read under it. They always surrounded by the colorful surrounded materials no matter the outside are. The Creative Zone creates and organizing various mind practices for organizations. In weekend and school holidays, there are many events such as coloring games, storytelling, fun and puppet shows.





I-Step is a major presence in the Interactive Zone which works to arouse the cheerful mood of a child. On-Location Reporter is an effective, live-journalists-style game app for public speaking. 360 Kinect XBOX games are also available, with a minimum charge of RM0.50 per session, to promote strategy thinking in video games.

Definitely the smart little guy does not want to miss the internet. Cyber Zone offers 8 Windows-based personal computers, and 8 iMac computers. Just charge RM1.00 per hour.
