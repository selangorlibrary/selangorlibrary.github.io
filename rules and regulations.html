Rules and Regulations


  1. Customers are not permitted to mark with a pen / pencil or fold book pages or in any other manner injurious to PPAS books, magazines, tools, furniture and furniture.

  2. Books and APDs are not allowed to be taken out of the library unless borrowed.

  3. Customers must notify staff in charge of all books to be taken out of the library..

  4. Customers are not allowed to make noises that may disturb other customers.

  5. Customers are not allowed to bring or sell food and drinks at the library. Books and APDs are not allowed to be taken out of the library unless borrowed.

  6. Customers are not allowed to eat, smoke and spit in the library.

  7. Customers should dress neatly when entering the library.

  8. Customers are not allowed to bring pets to the library.
